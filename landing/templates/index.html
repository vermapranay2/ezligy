Who are we and what do we do?

Ezligy is a LLP having an in-house highly qualified team of Advocates, Charted Accountants, Legal advisors and Para Legal manpower including Clerks,. Our team is committed for handling of all legal services and compliances in most efficient manner. We have a dedicated desk for advice, assistance and providing a right person to do the services which one can opt for through the forum to provide quick, easy and effective assistance in the most efficient manner with a time and money saving manner.

Any legal task is scrutinised at the back office at first by the team of our experts and practicing lawyers, it is then forwarded to the concerned para-legal expert of the field related to the task who gives a preliminary assessment over the procedure compliances as per applicable laws along with the professional time required and the estimate of the professional charges for the same.
