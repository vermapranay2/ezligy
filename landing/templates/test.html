For becoming a Non-Profit Organization(NPO), the license shall be obtained from Central Government and other conditions for obtaining license are as follows:

  1. The main objective is to promote arts, commerce, science, research, charity, social welfare, protection of environment, religion, education
  2. The income of NPO shall be utilized in promotion of its object and payment of dividend to its member is totally prohibited.

If Central Government is satisfied with the above mentioned conditions, then it shall grant the license. The word Ltd. or Pvt. Ltd. shall not be a part of its name.

  • Features Includes

    It is incorporated only for promotion of commerce,arts, science, sports, education, research, social welfare, religion, charity, protection of environment or any other such object and the profit or income shall be applied for the main object.
    It shall enjoy all the privileges, subject to all the obligations of Limited Company. NPO shall not be treated as Small Company.
    The application for registering the NPO shall be made to ROC under whose jurisdiction the registered office of the company is to be situated.
    Its income is taxable according to the rates applicable to the other companies.
    It shall not alter the provisions of its memorandum or articles except with the prior approval of Central Government.

  • Benefits

      Tax deductions to the donors of the company u/s 80 G of the Income Tax Act.
      Exemption of Stamp Duty for registration.
      Many privileges and exemptions are provided under Company Law.
      It can be formed with or without share capital, in case they are formed without capital,then necessary funds for carrying the business are brought in form of donations, subscriptions from members and general public.
      A partnership firm may become a member of NPO and membership shall cease upon dissolution of the firm. However, partners of the dissolved firm may continue to be the members of such company in their individual capacity.
      Easy transferability of ownership.

  • Limitations

      Profit of the company shall be applied for the promotion of the main object and no member shall be appointed as a remunerated officer.
      Declaration of dividend or distribution of profit to the promotes or members is not allowed.
      If conditions are not satisfied then license may be revoked but opportunity of being heard shall be given, if license is revoked then the word Ltd. or Pvt. Ltd.shall be a part of its name and all the exemptions shall be withdrawn.

  • Documents Required

    PAN Card, Address Proof (electricity bill/ water bill/ phone bill/ bank statement) ID Proof(Voter ID/ Aadhar Card/ Driving License/ Passport) is required of all directors and shareholders
    Registered office address proof (if rented, than rent agreement and NOC from the owner of premises) shall be obtained.
    Passport size photographs of all directors and shareholders.

  • Registration Procedure

    1 Minimum 2 shareholders and 2 directors shall be required to form Section 8 Company. The directors and shareholders can be same person.
    2 Director Identification Number (DIN) and Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) for all the directors shall be obtained from the Certifying Authority.
    3  Obtain Name Approval Letter from the ROC after making an application in prescribed form. Six names in preferential order may be proposed in single application.s
    4 Draft MOA and AOA of the proposed company according to the format given in Schedule 1 of the Companies Act, 2013.
    5  The MOA and AOA have to be signed by each of the subscribers indicating the no. of shares subscribed by each of them in MOA. The MOA should be in form INC 13. The AOA also mentions the names of the First Directors of the Company.
    6 An application for License under Section 8 of new companies with a charitable objective shall be filed in form INC 12 along with MOA and AOA and prescribed fee.
    7 Declaration in form INC 14 by an Advocate/ CA/ CS/ Cost Accountant in practice, that the MOA & AOA, rules and all requirements have been drawn up in conformity with Section 8
    8  After filing the requisite forms i.e, INC 7, INC 22 and DIR 12 for the incorporation with the ROC along with the prescribed fees, and ROC gets satisfied, then it shall issue Certificate of Incorporation in the prescribed form.

  • Frequenty Asked Questions

     What is the minimum capital requirement for incorporation of Section 8 Company?
    There is no requirement of minimum capital.
     Whether the provisions of CSR are applicable to Section 8 companies?
    Since, Section 8 apply their profits in promotion of its objects, therefore, it is not required to follow CSR provisions.
      Under which conditions license of Section 8 company may be revoked?
    If conditions and objects of obtaining the license are not satisfied then license shall be revoked but opportunity of being heard shall be given. If license is revoked then the word ltd. or pvt. Ltd. shall be the part of its name and all the exemptions shall be withdrawn.
      Is Stamp duty required on registration of Section 8 company?
    Company (NPO) is registered without paying any Stamp duty on its MOA & AOA.
      Who can become a member in Section 8 Company?
    Any individual / organization shall become a member including foreigners / NRI (details shall be obtained).
